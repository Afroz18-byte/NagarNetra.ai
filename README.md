# NagarNetra.ai
NagarNetra AI — AI-powered civic issue reporting  platform that turns citizen voices into government action.

NagarNetra AI (Eye of the City) is an AI-powered 
civic governance platform built for Indian cities. 
Citizens report local problems like potholes, water 
leakages, broken streetlights, and garbage dumps by 
simply taking a photo. Google Gemini Vision 
automatically analyzes the photo, categorizes the 
issue, assigns severity, and routes it to the correct 
government department. A multi-agent AI system with 
10 autonomous agents handles verification, duplicate 
detection, priority scoring, escalation, and resolution 
— making the entire process from report to resolution 
faster, transparent, and accountable.
